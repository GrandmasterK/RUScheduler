FAQs

What are subject/course/section numbers?

Go to my.rutgers.edu. Sign in and click on the Academics tab. From there on the top left you should see a table with your classes and in the course number column you should see a number such as 01:198:111. The second set of numbers,198, is the subject number while the third set, 111, is the course number. You can find the section number in the column right next to that, in the Section column.
